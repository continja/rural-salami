Details  

The show plays on-demand.

  • Tune your radio to 87.9FM
  • Text the word GO to the number displayed on the garage door
  • Sit back and enjoy!

Once triggered, the show will create a fresh, randomized playlist of about 30 songs. It will play a set of 6, then wait to be triggered again to play the next set. Rinse and repeat. If not triggered again within about 10 minutes, it will reset and be ready to create a new random playlist for the next visitor.

We know it’s a long show and most can’t/won’t stay for the whole thing. We play the show in shorter sets so that visitors can leave when they’ve had enough, and the show isn’t playing when there’s no one watching. And because the playlist is randomized for each new visitor, there’s a good chance you’ll see something different if you come back another night.
