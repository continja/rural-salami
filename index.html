Service Availability: On-demand is active only when you see the number displayed on the garage. If you don’t see it, the feature is off (outside of show hours or due to maintenance).

Details: How to Play On-Demand  

The show plays ON-DEMAND and controlled by you!

  1. Tune your radio to 87.9FM.
  2. Text go to the number displayed on the garage.

Once triggered, the show starts a new session with a unique, randomized playlist and plays the first set of songs.

  • For the next set: Text go again.
  • Variety Guaranteed: Because the show plays in short sets and randomizes the playlist for each new session, you won’t see repeat songs, and you can enjoy as much as you like!
  • Playlist Reset: If the show isn’t triggered for ~10 minutes, the session automatically resets for the next visitor.

For more control, like playing specific songs, check out the full 2025 Thanksgiving Show Page.
