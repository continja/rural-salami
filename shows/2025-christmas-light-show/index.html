You Control the Show  

1. SMS/Text (to the number on the garage)  

  • Random: Text go to randomize the playlist and play a short set. Repeat to play the next.
  • Specific Song: Text go followed by the song number (eg, go7 plays “Frosty the Snowman”).

2. Play from the Web (this page)  

  • Click the PLAY button next to any song in the playlist below.

More Info  

  • This feature is only active when the phone number is displayed on the garage.
  • Requests are blocked while a song is playing. You must wait for the current song to finish before requesting a new one.
  • This control is intended for in-person visitors only. You may be prompted to allow GPS location access to verify you are nearby.
